\jisec{Postpositions}
\jissec{\emph{i}, at, where (place)}
Following a noun phrase, it means ‘at’, and following a verb phrase, it means ‘where’.
\begin{description}
\item[Reosal dësistis’ü i üsaat’esh] I live in the Kingdom of Reosal
\item[Kula aal’a sap i dre’sh] I go to where the wind turns
\end{description}
